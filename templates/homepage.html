WHO WE ARE

JOO TIPS is a digital game academy that helps middle and high classes pupils, as well as students, learn the most common programming languages conveniently and without congestion.

Thanks to the convenient format of educational material, which is presented by small cards-tips and to the system of theoretical and practical tests, the user acquires the necessary knowledge and immediately learn to use them in practice.

Thanks to the innovative learning format, which consists of lessons from 5 to 30 minutes, combined with the most common mechanics of multiplayer games - individual and team matches, championships and challenges, users can distract from the routine of the classic learning process and focus on their own progress.

Users on the platform will be assisted by teachers who will organize the competition process and mentors who will share valuable knowledge and successful cases.
